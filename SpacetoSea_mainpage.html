This exhibition features the extraordinary diversity of marine life in New England's waters — from apex predators and ocean giants to massive schools of fish and microscopic alien-like plankton that are so numerous they are even visible from space. The awe-inspiring collection of photographs is a window into the underwater world of New England. Space to Sea features the extraordinary diversity of marine life in our local waters — from apex predators and ocean giants to massive schools of fish and microscopic alien-like plankton that are so numerous they are even visible from space. Keith Ellenbogen, renowned underwater photographer and recipient of an Ernest F. Hollings Ocean Awareness Award, brings us beneath the surface and beyond the clouds to discover the vibrant wildlife of Stellwagen Bank National Marine Sanctuary. Please join us in celebrating Stellwagen’s 30th anniversary and the marine wildlife it supports. Explore this free public photography exhibition by walking along the North and South sides of the New England Aquarium.
